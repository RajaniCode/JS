2.9 The Best Spot

As you exit the Inverted Peninsula, Aiedra floats beside you.

    "Tell me, Dhuun, of your knowledge of the script execution. What is the best location for a script tag, if it is a very large script file that will take much time to load and process."

Select your response.

    The top, just after the body of the page begins.

    The middle, but after other scripts have loaded.

    The bottom, just before the final body tag.

Answer: The bottom, just before the final body tag.
