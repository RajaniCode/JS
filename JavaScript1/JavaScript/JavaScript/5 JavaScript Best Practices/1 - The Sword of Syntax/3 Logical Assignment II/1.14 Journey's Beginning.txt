1.14 Journey's Beginning

"The Four Lands are threatened by the coming of Infinite Midnight," says Aiedra. "It is nearly upon us. You, Dhuun, must bring fire to the Legibility Lamps once more…"

Over and over Aiedra insists on your aid, till finally you agree to go on this quest she speaks of…to end the Infinite Midnight.

To begin your journey in this instance, however, there are two circumstances that need to be true.

var aiedraIsConvincing = true;
var dhuunIsCurious = true;

Use the variables provided above to logically assign a result to the beginJourney variable.


Answer: var beginJourney = aiedraIsConvincing && dhuunIsCurious;
