1.9 Special Characters in Strings

Build the following output in one String, using special characters for the newline, tab stop, and quotation marks:

Quoth the raven:
    "Nevermore!"

Answer: 

'Quoth the raven:' + '\n'  +  '\t' + '"Nevermore!"'

or

'Quoth the raven:\n\t"Nevermore!"'
