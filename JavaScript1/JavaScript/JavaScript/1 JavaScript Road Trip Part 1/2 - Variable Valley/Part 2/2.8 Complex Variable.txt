2.8 Complex Variable

Declare a variable called index14 and assign to it the character contained at that index in the string:

4_r t#f g; jw ? 3k2 1 !

Remember that you can assign a single character manually, or use a helpful method on the entire string.

Answer: 

var index14 = "4_r t#f g; jw ? 3k2 1 !".charAt(14);
